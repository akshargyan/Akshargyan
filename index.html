Working for a better Tomorrow...

As we all know the children are the roots of the future which is going to blossom and here we are providing them Education which is going to stay with them until their last breath, which is inexhaustible or which increases on sharing, which helps them to make a Heaven on Earth.
Education is a fundamental right of everyone (every person) & that’s also our main motto “Education for All’.

...If we want a better tomorrow we must inculcate humane values in children. We are giving them knowledge so that they are able to make a bridge to cross any hurdle. Education is not just a theory inside the book it’s knowledge, training, a tool to enhance the lives of children.

We teach we encourage, we help them to identify & sharpen their skills.
We are converting our free time into knowledge for them.

It not only soothers and freshers up the mind along with that it is equally important for our healthy health. Diwali, a festival of lights We Aksharyan members celebrate it with our students too. It starts with an introduction to diwali or speech on which later on, in succession questions are asked related to the speech and the correct answered students are prized. These prizes boost up the childrens to overcome fear of answering in public as well give them tastier chocolates, later we progress with Rangoli making, diya painting competitions and for small kids colouring is their. Each materials are provided by us to them and each one of them, are seen making their parts very elegant. Apart from these there is also nibandh writting competition. Bright faces could be seen. Everything is carried out in a very systematic manner with no chaos. Now comes the delicious moment, where childrens are served with delicious foods and then comes the time to take a leave. Childrens form lines and each student exiting the campus is "presented with sweets, inreturn giving their mesmirizing pleasant vote of thanks and smiles.
